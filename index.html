Research

I am a wildlife ecologist studying the effects of changing on wildlife. Warming winters are changing the way that animals move, eat, hunt, and sleep. Using wildlife cameras, we can detect both where animals are occuring and the amount of snow. Connecting the two together allows us to explore questions like, "How is snow changing where deer move? When they move? How might this affect interactions with other animals in the landscape, like wolves and lynx?"
